Add a GE to a course

Choose a course and enter a duckid to associate a GE. The process which sends GEs into canvas only runs once a day, so you may have to wait up to 24 hours for your form submission to take effect.

Note that you may associate a GE to the same course multiple times and the import will still function properly. If you aren't sure if you've entered somebody, there's no risk in entering them again.
