Good news! The ability to add other users to your course is now available within Canvas. This self-serve process is live as of 9:00am, April 5, 2019. New roles are available within Canvas. Add a TA / Grader to a course

Enter a duckid, choose a course, and choose a role (TA or Grader). This process will send enrollments to Canvas three times daily. If you aren't sure if you've entered somebody, there's no risk in entering them again.
